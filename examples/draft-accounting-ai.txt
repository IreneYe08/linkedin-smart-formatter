As a PM at Cortex Workspace, I've spent months speaking with accountants and finance teams about how AI fits into their daily work.
One pattern appears repeatedly.
Accountants rarely struggle with finding an answer.
They spend most of their time gathering the information required to answer the question.
A typical workflow might involve:
- Pulling numbers from an ERP
- Searching through emails
- Reviewing internal policies
- Drafting client-facing summaries
Many AI tools help with bookkeeping, tax research, and document extraction.
**The remaining challenge is workflow fragmentation.**
During our research for the "Best AI Tools for Accountants 2026" report, this theme surfaced again and again:
Productivity gains come from reducing information retrieval and context-switching, not only from automating calculations.
Curious what others are seeing:
For accountants, finance teams, auditors, and consultants—where do you spend the most time today?
